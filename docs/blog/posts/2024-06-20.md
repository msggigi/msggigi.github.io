# Network Knowledge

* Search subnet for devices

        nmap -sn 192.168.1.0/24
        
* Show ports and services opened at localhost

        nmap -v -sV localhost
        
  if your internet provider is blocking ping reqs
  
        nmap -v -sV -Pn f<domain.url>
